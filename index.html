Understanding This and That

💡 The Grammar Rule:

  • 📍 Use THIS when an object is close to you (Near).
  • 🔭 Use THAT when an object is far away from you (Far).

THIS ... (Near)

☝️ Me

... THAT (Far)

🔭 Me
